Summary: SORTIQ is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data, store everything locally on your device, and never share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

SORTIQ collects and stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Game Progress: Completed levels, scores, and statistics
  • Preferences: Sound settings, haptic feedback preferences, language selection
  • Achievements: Unlocked achievements and progress
  • Streak Data: Daily playing streak information

This data is stored locally using your device's secure storage and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. Advertising

SORTIQ displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect:

  • Device identifiers for ad personalization
  • General location (country/region level) for relevant ads
  • Ad interaction data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

3. In-App Purchases

SORTIQ offers a "Remove Ads" purchase option. All payment processing is handled by Google Play Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

4. Data Storage

All your game data is stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app, your progress will be deleted. We do not offer cloud backup functionality.

5. Children's Privacy

SORTIQ is a general audience game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game does not require account creation or personal data entry.

6. Third-Party Services

SORTIQ uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Play Billing: For in-app purchases

7. Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features. Since we don't transmit data to external servers, there is no risk of data breach from our side.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all data: Use the "Reset Progress" option in Settings
  • Disable personalized ads: Adjust settings on your device
  • Uninstall the app: This removes all locally stored data

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
