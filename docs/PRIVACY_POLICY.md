# Privacy Policy for SORTIQ

**Last updated: February 8, 2026**

## Introduction

SORTIQ ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information**: We may collect device-specific information such as device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and advertising purposes.

**Usage Data**: We collect information about how you interact with the app, including levels completed, game progress, and session duration.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- Personal identification information (name, email, address)
- Location data
- Contact information
- Photos or media files

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain our game service
- Save your game progress locally on your device
- Display personalized advertisements
- Improve our app and user experience
- Analyze usage patterns and app performance

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data as described in [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google's Ad Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads).

### In-App Purchases

We use Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. Payment information is handled directly by Google Play and is not stored by us.

## Data Storage

All game progress and settings are stored locally on your device using Hive database. We do not store any personal data on external servers.

## Children's Privacy

SORTIQ is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require registration or personal information to play.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Delete all app data by uninstalling the application
- Opt out of personalized advertising through device settings
- Contact us with any privacy concerns

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
